Naïve Bayesian Estimator running in STM32L053R8Tx [ARM Cortex M0+] with an interval of 10 Mills feeding new values with the help of HAL_RNG.
Added Blink program just to keep an Eye on LED that helps in conforming if the controller is up and running fine or not. 

functionality:
 - Clock Settings:
   - XTAL    = HSI     = 16.00 MHz
   - SYSCLK  =           32.00 MHz
